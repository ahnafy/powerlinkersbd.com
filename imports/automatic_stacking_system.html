Details description of Automatic Brick stacking system:

Brick stacking system is consisting of brick separating and transporting system and brick stacker. Brick separating and transporting system coordinate with brick stacker to turn, group and transport the bricks. Brick stacker is used for stacking the brick on the kiln cart, according to the structure, it can be divided to automatic stacker and automatic robot stacker.
