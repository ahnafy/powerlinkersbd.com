Details description of Biomass Pellet Machine:

Biomass pellet machine is your best choice for processing such biomass materials as wood, sawdust, crop straw, peanut shell and cotton stalk into pellets for pellet stove, pellet boiler or power plant. Biomass pellets can improve burning efficiency, ease of transportation, energy saving and environment protection.

The Biomass pellet mills are patented products with unique design by Shandong Double Crane Machinery Manufacturer Co., Ltd, the professional biomass wood pellet machine producer in China. The biomass pellet mill is one of the key machine in the biomass pellet production line.

Product Features:

  • ●The finished pellets can keep floating for 3-15 hours without pollution to water./li>
  • ●The floating time can be controlled by the puffing degree adjustment.
  • ●Floating fish feed machine can make pellet from dia.1.0mm-20mm just by changing the mold.
